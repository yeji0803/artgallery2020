Wallflower

This is an ongoing collection of work inspired by my collection of unique vintage wallpaper.
It is a continuing investigation of femininity as a beautiful force that exists strongly and joyfully on its own, outside the need for relevance of the male gaze.
I am particularly focussed on empowerment through beauty.
